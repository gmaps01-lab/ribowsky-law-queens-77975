What are your fees for handling slip and fall cases in New York?
Ribowsky Law works on a contingency fee basis for slip and fall cases, which means you pay no upfront costs or hourly rates. Our fee is a percentage of the compensation we recover for you, and if we don’t win your case, you owe us nothing. If you have questions about how the percentage is calculated or what expenses might be deducted from your settlement, we’re happy to provide a clear breakdown during your consultation.

The timeline for a slip and fall case in NYC can vary based on the specifics of your case. Factors that can affect timing include the severity of your injuries, how quickly evidence can be gathered, and the willingness of the property owner or insurer to negotiate.
